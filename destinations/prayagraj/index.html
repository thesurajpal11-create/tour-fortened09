About Destination

Prayagraj Overview

Prayagraj is a major pilgrimage destination known for the Triveni Sangam, where the Ganga, Yamuna, and the mystical Saraswati are believed to meet. It holds special significance for devotees and is frequently part of Ayodhya and Varanasi circuits. Visitors often come for Sangam snan, temple stops, fort views, and spiritually focused family journeys. Ramnagari Tourism provides route planning, cab booking, stay assistance, and same-day or overnight package support.
